Rich Culture

Traditional Dance & Music

Rwanda's cultural heritage is preserved through vibrant dances like the Intore, which was once performed for the royal court.

Cuisine

Try local dishes like Ugali, Isombe (cassava leaves), and Brochettes (meat skewers) paired with local Urwagwa (banana beer).

Crafts

Rwanda is known for its beautiful handwoven baskets called "Agaseke," which symbolize peace and prosperity.
